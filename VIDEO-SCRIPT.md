## The 30-second elevator version (if you need a short cut)
> Attackers don't break in — they log in. Identity is the number-one attack surface, but the work to defend it is invisible and tedious, so it never gets done. **Afterlogin** turns it into a game: every forgotten account is a ghost you have until dawn to judge. Your AI agents reason over the real signals — grounded and cited by **Foundry IQ**, dependency-mapped by **Fabric IQ** — and the twist *is* the lesson: not every dead account is safe to delete. ## Production tips (how to make it look pro)
- **Cinematic Mode for free:** press **🎬 Hunt Night** and it auto-hides the HUD + adds letterbox — perfect hands-free b-roll while you narrate. Press it again to take back control.
- **Record VO separately** and lay it over the footage; cut the dead air between agent steps.
- **Show the build stamp** (mode-select screen) once so it's clearly the live build.
- **Give the three acts real screen time** — switching acts on camera is what proves breadth across Entra/Purview/Defender.
- **Land two beats hardest:** the *agents + IQ grounding* (0:42–1:30) and the *load-bearing/poltergeist* lesson (1:30–2:00). Those are the unique, memorable, and judge-relevant moments.
- 1080p/1440p, 60fps if you can. End on the **sunrise + scoreboard** — never trail off into a menu.

## The judging checklist this script hits
