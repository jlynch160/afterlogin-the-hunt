# Afterlogin — Demo Video Script & Shot List

**Target: ~3:15 (hard cap 5:00). Tighter scores better.**

## Before you record
- Open the live site, **enter the password**, hard-refresh once.
- Record at **1080p or 1440p**, 60fps if possible.
- For clean gameplay shots: press **C** (Cinematic Mode) — hides HUD, adds letterbox.
- For hands-free b-roll: click **Autopilot** first, *then* press **C** — narrate over it.
- Have a run already in progress (a few ghosts judged) so the board looks alive.
- To show the acts: from the Sanctum, switch **Act I → II → III** so each looks distinct on camera.
- Record VO separately and lay it over the footage.

## Tips
- **One-liner is everything.** "Attackers don't break in — they log in" earns attention in the first 5 seconds.
- Lead with the **agents** and the **load-bearing/poltergeist** beat — the unique, memorable insight.
- Give the **three acts** real screen time (2:00–2:40) — switching acts on camera shows breadth across Entra/Purview/Defender.
- The **expansions tease** signals ambition without over-promising — keep it to one breath.
- End on the **sunrise**. Don't trail off or show a menu.
- ~470 words of VO ≈ 3:10 at a natural pace. Cut, don't pad.
