# Afterlogin — Demo Video Script & Shot List

**Target: ~3:00 (hard cap 5:00). Tighter scores better.**

## Before you record
- Open the live site, **enter the password**, hard-refresh once.
- Record at **1080p or 1440p**, 60fps if possible.
- For clean gameplay shots: press **C** (Cinematic Mode) — hides HUD, adds letterbox.
- For hands-free b-roll: click **Autopilot** first, *then* press **C** — narrate over it.
- Have a run already in progress (a few ghosts judged) so the board looks alive.
- Record VO separately and lay it over the footage; don't rely on game audio for narration.

## The cut (timecode · shot · narration)

### 0:00–0:18 — HOOK (title)
**Shot:** Homepage — let the title "Afterlogin" do its staggered reveal. Hold on the card.
**VO:** "Attackers don't break in anymore. They log in. Identity is the number-one attack surface in security — and the hardest thing to govern, because the work is invisible and tedious. So we made it a game you actually *want* to play."

### 0:18–0:50 — CONCEPT (the manor)
**Shot:** Click *Enter the Manor* → the map appears. Press **C** for Cinematic Mode. Slow pan across the floor; let a haunted room's wisps and the soul-stars show.
**VO:** "This is Afterlogin. Every forgotten identity in your tenant — a dormant admin, an orphaned service account, a mis-consented app — is a ghost haunting this manor. You're the auditor, and you have until dawn to judge them all… before something *hungry* reaches the Vault."

### 0:50–1:45 — THE AGENTS (your money shot)
**Shot:** Click a room → the dossier opens (exit Cinematic with **C** so the panel reads). Click **Divine** (threads reveal), **Summon** (evidence). Let the **Retriever → Warden → Skeptic → Council** reasoning stream and light up.
**VO:** "Click any ghost, and your party of AI agents goes to work. The **Warden** reasons over the real identity signals; the **Skeptic** contests it; together they reach a verdict. But here's the twist that's also the lesson — *not every dead account is safe to delete.*"
**Shot:** Pick a **load-bearing** ghost (e.g. the nightly job / break-glass) and choose **Lay to Rest** → the **poltergeist** fires (red shockwave, Ledger drops).
**VO:** "Some are load-bearing — quietly powering a live process. Exorcise one… and you've just broken production. That's the poltergeist. The whole game teaches the difference between a dead account and a load-bearing one."

### 1:45–2:15 — THE THREAT + PAYOFF
**Shot:** The Hungry on the map advancing toward the Vault. Click it → dice combat, the Sentinel strikes. Then make a clean **Lay to Rest** → the soul rises into a **star**.
**VO:** "Meanwhile, a live account-takeover hunts the halls toward Tier-0. You fight it alongside your agents with dice-based Microsoft Graph remediations. And every soul you lay to rest rises into the sky — your night, written in stars."

### 2:15–2:40 — DEPTH (campaign + rogue-lite)

**VO:** "It's a full campaign across the Microsoft security stack — identity in **Entra**, data in **Purview**, the breach in **Defender** — each act harder than the last. Fall, bank Soul Essence, buy permanent upgrades, and return stronger. Every night rolls different omens. It's a rogue-lite."

### 2:40–3:00 — COPILOT + CLOSE

**Shot:** Trigger the **Dawn Breaks** finale — hold on the sunrise + the rank.
**VO:** "It's a game… but point it at your tenant, and the ghosts are your real attack surface. Hold until dawn."

## Tips
- **One-liner is everything.** "Attackers don't break in — they log in" earns attention in the first 5 seconds.
- Lead with the **agents** and the **load-bearing/poltergeist** beat — that's the unique, memorable insight.
- Don't explain every system; *show* depth in fast cuts (2:15–2:40).
- End on the **sunrise**. Don't trail off or show a menu.
- ~420 words of VO ≈ 2:50 at a natural pace. Cut, don't pad.
