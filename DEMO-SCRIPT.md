# Afterlogin — Demo video script (≤ 5 min)

Target ~3:30. Record in a **wide browser window** (not an embedded preview) for the full
bloom. Open `index.html?v=demo` fresh, mouse ready on **Begin the Night**. Your own
voice/screen only (rules: the video must be solely your own work — no licensed music unless royalty-free/yours).

> Tip: do a silent dry-run first so the on-screen beats land on your narration.

---

### 0:00–0:20 · Hook + problem
**Do:** Title screen visible.
**Say:** *"Every company is haunted — by the accounts it forgot. Dormant service principals,
orphaned admins, stale privileges. They're invisible, they're dangerous, and they're the
first thing attackers hunt. So I turned identity-governance triage into a game you actually
want to play. This is Afterlogin — and it's built with GitHub Copilot, grounded by
Foundry IQ."*
**Do:** Click **Begin the Night**. Let the **Act I** card and opening narration play.

### 0:20–0:40 · Meet the party (agents)
**Do:** Point cursor at the **top-right Party roster**.
**Say:** *"I don't play alone. I have two AI hunter agents. A **Warden** that reasons about
each account — and a **Sentinel** that fights alongside me. Watch their status here, live."*

### 0:40–1:25 · The reasoning flow (the money shot)
**Do:** Click the **Cellar** ghost (the F-grade `adm-legacy-backup`). The world **freezes**,
the centered modal pops.
**Say:** *"Click a ghost and everything pauses so I can think. Up top — the **IQ pipeline**."*
**Do:** Click **Divine** (step 2 pops, green). *"**Fabric IQ** — the identity ontology —
shows me the threads: is it still bound to anything alive?"*
**Do:** Click **Summon** (step 3 pops). *"**Foundry IQ** pulls **cited evidence** — the
runbooks, the CMDB, the policy. Grounded, not guessed."*
**Do:** Let the **Warden analysis** stream → recommendation appears. *"And the Warden
reasons over it and recommends the right call — here, **Lay to Rest**, because it's truly
dead. Notice the recommendation **matches** the glowing button."*
**Do:** Click **Lay to Rest**. *"Resolved."*

### 1:25–2:00 · The twist + stakes
**Do:** Click the **Boiler Room** (`svc-billing-reconcile`, F). Divine → show the **green
LIVE thread**.
**Say:** *"But not every dead-looking account is safe. This one has a **LIVE** thread — a
nightly job still calls it. If I exorcise it…"*
**Do:** Click **Lay to Rest** anyway → **poltergeist** detonates (shake, red cascade).
**Say:** *"…I break production. That tension — cited, grounded, human-judged — is the whole
point."* *(Restart if needed: "Walk the halls again.")*

### 2:00–2:45 · The Hunt — D&D combat
**Do:** Judge another ghost to trigger the **Hunt** (or continue until the monster appears).
**Say:** *"Take two turns and something starts **hunting** the ghosts — an account takeover,
a real monster with **HP and AC**, advancing toward the Vault every round."*
**Do:** Click the **red monster** → **Summon dossier** → **Smite**.
**Say:** *"I roll a d20 to strike — and my **Sentinel rolls too**. Agent and human, fighting
together, turn by turn."* (Call out a roll: *"Seventeen — hit!"*) Smite until **banished**.

### 2:45–3:10 · Autopilot (hands-free agents)
**Do:** Restart, then click **🤖 Autopilot**.
**Say:** *"And the agents can run the whole night themselves."* (Stop talking ~5 seconds and
let it play — ghosts judged, dice rolled, monster banished, in the log.)
**Say:** *"That's autonomous agentic reasoning, end to end."*

### 3:10–3:35 · Built for Copilot + Foundry IQ (the proof)
**Do:** Cut to **VS Code · GitHub Copilot Chat** with the MCP server registered.
**Say:** *"Same agents, headless. Through an **MCP server**, GitHub Copilot plays the manor
itself."*
**Do:** Type in Copilot Chat: *"List the rooms, divine the Boiler Room, and only lay it to
rest if it's truly dead."* Show the tool calls + the **Foundry IQ-grounded, cited** evidence.
**Say:** *"And `summon_evidence` is real **Foundry IQ** — permission-aware, cited retrieval.
Built with Copilot, built for Copilot, grounded by Foundry IQ. That's Afterlogin."*
